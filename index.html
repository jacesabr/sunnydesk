Works with what you already run

No API? No problem.

Most hotel software was never built to plug into an AI. Ours doesn't need it to. We put a human-sounding agent in front of whatever you already use — and where there's no integration, the agent operates the very same screens your staff do.

  • Any PMS or booking engine — we integrate by API when one exists, and drive the screens directly when one doesn't.
  • Legacy or in-house systems — even a decades-old desktop tool with no export can sit behind the agent.
  • The same engine runs the kiosk — the check-in screen and RFID card cutter talk to your system the same way.
  • Grounded, never guessing — every room, price and date the agent says comes from a real check against your system.

What we build

Four steps. Take one, or take them all.

Every module works alone and plugs into what you already run.

MODULE 01

Website concierge

One line of code, no rebuild. It asks what the stay is for and shows real rooms as photo cards.

  • Live rates from your booking engine — never invented
  • Voice or chat, any language — escalates to staff when it should
MODULE 02

Phone agent

Every call answered on the first ring, 24/7 — by a human-sounding voice that's still enthusiastic at 3am. No voicemail, no missed bookings.

  • Books rooms and answers the routine 80% — parking, breakfast, check-in times
  • Complex requests are recorded and queued for a staff callback — with full context
MODULE 03

Check-in kiosk + RFID cards

Under a minute: the kiosk finds the reservation, runs your check-in steps, and encodes the key card on the spot.

  • ID, payment, registration — whatever your jurisdiction requires
  • Midnight arrivals stop depending on who's at the desk
MODULE 04

RFID locks & hardware install

Locks, encoders and cards — sourced and installed through vetted local suppliers, wherever your hotel is.

  • Installed by local partners who can come back the same week
  • Or we integrate with the lock system you already have
